# UI Review Checklist

## Workflow

- Is the active job or artifact obvious without explanatory text?
- Can the user see the current step and next action?
- Can the user revise one section without regenerating everything?
- Are sources and unresolved facts visible near the draft?
- Are loading, empty, failed, needs-input, and completed states implemented?

## Visual Quality

- Is the interface a workbench rather than a landing page or chat demo?
- Are page sections unframed and cards reserved for repeated artifacts?
- Are colors neutral and varied enough to avoid a one-hue AI aesthetic?
- Are controls compact, aligned, and stable when content changes?
- Do long Chinese titles and file names wrap or truncate safely?

## Safety And Trust

- Is privacy class visible before external processing?
- Does export require deliberate approval?
- Are generated facts linked to evidence?
- Can the user distinguish confirmed facts, inference, and opinion?
- Are destructive or irreversible actions clearly confirmed?

## Responsive Behavior

- On narrow screens, does the evidence panel collapse without covering the editor?
- Do toolbars wrap or move into menus without overlapping content?
- Do file names, statuses, and primary actions remain readable?
