# Topic Scoring Prompt

Please score the following topic out of 10.

## Scoring Dimensions

1. Does it hit a real business owner pain point?
2. Does it show my professional differentiation?
3. Is it likely to be saved or shared?
4. Can it naturally lead to a diagnostic service?
5. Is there enough evidence?
6. Does it avoid sensitive or unverifiable claims?
7. Can I keep producing similar topics at this stage?

## Output

- Total score
- Recommended platform
- Recommended format
- Revision suggestions
- Whether it should enter this week's content plan
