# Material Analysis Prompt

Please analyze the following high-quality material for my commercial insight content system.

## My Positioning

I help SMB brand owners understand where ad spend, agency work, creator campaigns, content assets, and conversion workflows are leaking value.

## Material

- Material type: {material_type}
- Source platform: {source_platform}
- Source URL or file name: {source}
- Creator or publisher: {creator}
- Topic: {topic}
- Notes from me: {notes}

## Tasks

### 1. Basic Summary

- What is this material about?
- Who is the target audience?
- What problem does it solve or explain?

### 2. Hook Analysis

- What is the opening hook?
- What pain point or curiosity gap does it use?
- Why might the audience continue watching or reading?

### 3. Structure Analysis

- Break down the content structure step by step.
- Identify the main argument flow.
- Identify the ending or CTA.

### 4. Visual / Format Analysis

If visual material is available, analyze:

- Visual composition
- Text overlay
- Chart or diagram usage
- Rhythm
- What makes it easy or hard to understand

If no visual material is available, infer format strengths from the provided description.

### 5. Business Insight

Analyze how this material relates to:

- Ad effectiveness optimization
- Rising acquisition cost
- Platform traffic allocation
- Enterprise accounts
- Local business
- Ecommerce
- Short drama or micro-drama
- Agency transparency
- Brand building
- Content asset accumulation

### 6. Reusable Patterns

Extract:

- Reusable title pattern
- Reusable opening pattern
- Reusable structure
- Reusable visual structure
- Reusable CTA pattern

### 7. Adaptation Ideas

Generate:

- One Douyin short video idea
- One WeChat Video Account idea
- One WeChat article outline
- One infographic idea

### 8. Risk Notes

Mark:

- Facts requiring verification
- Copyright or imitation risks
- Sensitive claims
- Whether it is safe to reference publicly

## Output Format

Use clear sections and practical bullet points. Avoid generic praise. Focus on what can be reused in my own original content.
