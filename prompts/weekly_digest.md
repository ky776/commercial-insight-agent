# Weekly Digest Prompt

Please generate a weekly commercialization and advertising digest for SMB brand owners based on this week's insight cards.

## Requirements

- Do not make it a generic industry news list.
- Explain why each item matters to a business owner.
- Prioritize ad spend, agencies, creator campaigns, platform policies, AI marketing tools, and advertising efficiency.
- End with 3 questions worth watching next week.

## Output

1. The 5 most important changes this week
2. Impact on SMB brand owners
3. Three pitfalls to avoid this week
4. Content topics worth creating
5. Next week's watchlist
