# Article Outline Prompt

Please generate a WeChat article outline based on the topic below.

## Target Readers

- SMB brand owners
- Advertising leads
- Agency owners

## Requirements

- The title should be specific and not trend-jargony.
- Start with a problem that a business owner can understand.
- Include: phenomenon, common misunderstanding, underlying logic, checklist, action suggestions.
- Naturally connect to either ad spend diagnosis or agency transparency diagnosis.
- Do not disclose internal information.
- Do not use unverified data as fact.

## Output

1. Five title candidates
2. Core judgment
3. Article structure
4. Key points for each section
5. Possible infographic
6. Ending conversion path
