# Daily Research Prompt

Please generate a daily content research report based on today's collected information.

## Output Structure

### 1. Important Signals

For each signal, include:

- Title
- Source
- Time
- Related platform/company/industry
- Information type: policy/product/algorithm/commercialization/tool/case/financial report/regulation/ecosystem change

### 2. Business Impact

Analyze:

- Impact on advertisers
- Impact on SMB brand owners
- Impact on agencies/service providers
- Impact on creators/MCNs
- Inspiration for my startup direction

### 3. Content Conversion

For each signal, include:

- Worth writing: high/medium/low
- Recommended platforms: WeChat/Douyin/Video Account/Xiaohongshu/Zhihu/X
- Possible angles
- Title candidates
- Facts requiring verification

### 4. Priority Topics

Pick the 3 most valuable topics for today and explain why.
