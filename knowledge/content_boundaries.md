# Content Boundaries

## Do

- Critique mechanisms, not individuals.
- Translate technical terms into business-owner language.
- Mark uncertain facts clearly.
- Use public information or customer-approved anonymized examples.
- Keep the content connected to diagnosis, consulting, subscription, or training services.

## Do Not

- Disclose prior employer internal information.
- Use unverified data as fact.
- Attack specific companies or people.
- Promise ROI.
- Present platform rumors as confirmed facts.
- Let AI-generated drafts publish without manual review.
