# Opinion Bank

This file stores reusable opinions and hypotheses for content creation. Each opinion should be tested through content, customer conversations, and service delivery.

## Core Thesis

Advertising is becoming harder for SMB brands because traffic costs are rising, platform traffic allocation is changing, and simple paid acquisition is no longer enough.

Brands need to understand not only ad accounts, but also content assets, business accounts, local scenarios, ecommerce conversion, short drama content, and brand trust.

## Opinion 1: Ad Optimization Is No Longer Only Account Optimization

Many business owners still treat advertising optimization as changing bids, audiences, creatives, and campaign structures inside an ad account.

That is no longer enough.

The real optimization surface is expanding to:

- Ad account structure
- Content production efficiency
- Brand account operation
- Landing page and transaction path
- Customer follow-up workflow
- Creator and merchant collaboration
- Platform-native content assets

Content angle:

- 老板不要只问投手怎么调账户，要问内容资产有没有越投越值钱。
- 如果广告费只买到一次点击，而没有沉淀内容、账号、品牌和人群资产，成本一定越来越难控。

## Opinion 2: Rising Acquisition Cost Forces Brands Into Content Ecosystems

When platform traffic becomes more expensive, SMB brands cannot rely only on paid traffic.

Platforms are encouraging companies to participate more deeply in content ecosystems because platforms also need better supply:

- Better merchant content
- More local business content
- More ecommerce-native content
- More business account activity
- More short drama and micro-drama content supply
- More creator-business collaboration

Content angle:

- 平台不是单纯让你投广告，而是在逼企业变成内容生态的一部分。
- 未来很多老板不是不会投放，而是没有内容资产可以被平台分发。

## Opinion 3: Platform Traffic Resources Are Tilting Toward Business-Native Scenarios

Platform traffic allocation is increasingly connected with commercial scenarios, including:

- Enterprise accounts
- Local life services
- Ecommerce
- Short drama
- Micro-drama
- Creator commerce
- Private-domain follow-up

This means platforms are not only selling traffic. They are shaping business behavior.

Content angle:

- 平台流量不是平均分配的，它会流向更容易形成交易、履约和复购的场景。
- 老板要关心平台正在扶持什么，而不是只关心今天哪个计划跑得快。

## Opinion 4: Agency Transparency Becomes More Important When Systems Become More Complex

As platforms add more automated delivery, content signals, business account assets, and cross-scenario conversion paths, agencies can become harder to evaluate.

The owner needs a clearer diagnosis framework:

- What did the agency change?
- What did the platform change automatically?
- What did the content team contribute?
- What did the sales or operations team fail to follow up?
- Which metric improved because of budget, and which improved because of structure?

Content angle:

- 自动化投放越强，老板越需要透明度。
- 代理商只给结果截图，不解释过程和结构，就是风险信号。

## Opinion 5: Brand Building Is Becoming a Cost Control Strategy

For SMB brands, brand building is often seen as optional or expensive.

But when acquisition costs rise, basic brand trust can reduce waste:

- Higher conversion from the same click
- Better creator collaboration efficiency
- Stronger repeat purchase and referral
- More reliable organic distribution
- More stable customer decision-making

Content angle:

- 品牌不是大公司的奢侈品，而是中小企业控制获客成本的基础设施。
- 没有品牌信任，广告费会不断被用来弥补用户的不信任。

## Open Questions To Validate

- Which industries feel ad cost pressure most strongly right now?
- Which platform scenario is easiest for SMB brands to enter first: enterprise account, local, ecommerce, short drama, or creator commerce?
- Are business owners willing to pay for diagnosis before they pay for execution?
- What evidence convinces owners that agency transparency is a real management problem?
- Can content asset diagnosis become a standardized service package?
